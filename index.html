About

Hello! My name is Alexandra Kharina. I am 20 years old and I do graphic design, photography and illustration.

I graduated MAHL RAH and Foundation Art & Design at British Higher School of Art and Design.

Right now I am level 5 BA (Hons) Graphic Design student at British Higher School of Art and Design.

I worked at Movie Communication group as junior designer, HELLO! Magazine as a layout designer, Salon Magazine as an intern.

If you have some project and if you want to set it right — leave it to me. I’ll take charge of and put my heart into it.
